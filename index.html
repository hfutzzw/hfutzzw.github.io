About Me

I am currently a graduate student at Dept. Electronic Engineering & Information Science (EEIS) of University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My research interest includes deep learning, computer vision. At present, I focus on the Person Re-identification (Re-ID) and multimodal learning, co-supervised by associate professor Liu Bin and professor Nenghai Yu.
